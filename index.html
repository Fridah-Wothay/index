How Glovo Kenya Operates

Every few seconds Glovo algorithm looks at all the new orders in the city and all the available courriers and calculates the optimum order courrier, assignment so that the consumer receives their order courrie as quickly as possible. Glovo algorithm is called Jarvis.

What makes Glovo Kenya different

Glovo platform allows local stores to sell their products and services to users in the cities.

Ojectives of Glovo Kenya

  1. Gives everyone acesss to anything in their city.
  2. Whilst having a positive impact on the economy, society and environment.
